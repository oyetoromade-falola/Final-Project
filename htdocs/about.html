About COURSEMART FCFMT

Mission

To transform the academic experience at FCFMT through a smart, user-friendly platform that gives students access to department courses and relevant materials.

Vision

To be the leading digital course store in Nigerian colleges, offering intelligent recommendations powered by big data metrics and modern technology.

Learning Made Easy

COURSEMART showcases all academic courses across departments like ICT, Fisheries, Nautical Science, and more. Students can browse, filter, and add these to their cart, while the system recommends other useful content based on their interest, This also highlights professional courses offers in the college which indeed made laerning Super Easy for students and professionals in the industry.
